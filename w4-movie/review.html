REVIEW of the game
Destiny 2


Andrew Johnson, February 4th, 2024

★ ★ ★ ★ ☆

The Destiny franchise has been a part of my life since September 2015 and has continued to be my #1 favorite game of all time since the first day I booted it up. I fell in love with the looter-shooter style that the game possesed and the constant satisfaction of getting a new weapon or piece of armor combined with the replayability of the First-Person Shooter style. Its very deep and convoluted story that continues to grow through the franchises "10-year plan" have also been the reason I continue to invest time and money into this game. As difficult as it is to watch Bungie make terrible marketing and community appeal choices concerning a game that I hold very dearly and see its own playerbase turn against it. But no matter what happens to the game in the future, it will always be an important part of my life.

Destiny 2 Trivia:

  • Who is Currently the Main Antagonist of Destiny 2? Answer: The Witness
  • What is the name given to those that are resurrected by The Traveler to protect humanity? Answer: Guardians
  • Who was the Hunter vanguard for the last city that was killed in the begining of the "Forsaken" expansion? Answer: Cayde-6
