Destiny 2

"Destiny 2" is a multiplayer online first-person shooter game set in a mythic science fiction world where players, known as Guardians, embark on various missions to protect the last city on Earth and the Traveler, a celestial being, from various alien threats.

Production Company: Bungie

Release Date: September 6, 2017
